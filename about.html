ABOUT THE COMPANY

Founded in 2025, S2W ESPORTS is a platform with the sole aim of powering Esports experiences while helping grassroots community development in India and other South Asian Countries. The Entire Team behind S2W ESPORTS eats, sleeps & breathes gaming with over 20+ years of combined gaming, esports & marketing experience. The founding principle behind S2W ESPORTS was to provide gamers a platform to pursue their esports dreams while allowing brands to leverage long term & sustainable esports IP's. Our experienced team brings unique insight into the world of esports, allowing us to ensure we provide aspiring gamers with the best possible experience and every possible opportunity regardless of their choice of platform or game.
